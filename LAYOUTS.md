**Features:** Advanced TVPaint functions, Keyframe controls, Brush operations
**Note:** Thumb keys passthrough to base layer (DEL/BSPC/CMD on left, ENTER/SPACE/NAVI on right)

## Combos

- **Q + W** (positions 0+1): ESC
- **Z + X + C** (positions 11+12+13): Toggle TVP1 layer

## Macros

- **#gif**: Types "#gif" (Shift+2, G, I, F)

## Notes

- Base layout: Colemak-DH
- Home row mods with 170ms tapping term
- Tap-preferred flavor for better typing experience
- Quick tap: 100ms
- Global quick tap enabled
